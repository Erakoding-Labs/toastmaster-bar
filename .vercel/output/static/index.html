Our Story

A True Rock Springs Dive

The Toastmaster Bar & Liquor Store has been Rock Springs' go-to spot for a good night out — the kind of place where the bartender remembers your drink and the regulars save you a seat.

Shoot a round of pool, throw some darts, grab the mic on karaoke night, or just post up at the bar with a cold one. We're open late until 2 AM, so we're here when everywhere else has called it.

No frills, no attitude — just good drinks, good people, and the best dive-bar energy in town.
